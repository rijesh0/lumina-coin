How to Play Coin Flip Game

Bet your LUMINA and flip the coin. If the coin lands on your chosen side, you win double your bet. If not, you lose your bet.

1. Enter the amount of LUMINA you want to bet.
2. Click "Flip Coin" to make your wager.
3. If you win, your balance is doubled. Otherwise, you lose the bet.
